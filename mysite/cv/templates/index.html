Draw Platformer

Draw Your Own Platformer Video Game

Draw Platformer combines gaming and art: it allows you to unleash your creativity by drawing your own level of a platformer style video game. Using simple rules like black lines means a wall or drawing green circles creates coins, you can sketch your own level of a platformer video game on paper. Upload your drawing to our website, and using computer vision, we convert your masterpiece into a playable platformer game. Share your custom level with friends and compete for the highest score.
